Quick Tips

Field Guide
Co-pay Percentage

The portion patient pays directly. Enter as decimal:

  • 0.10 = 10% patient, 90% insurance
  • 0.20 = 20% patient, 80% insurance
  • 0.00 = Insurance pays 100%
Credit Limit

Maximum amount the provider can owe before payment is required. Set based on trust and contract terms.
